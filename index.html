About me

I am expected to receive my Ph.D. in Design at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in September 2025 and am currently seeking faculty opportunities. My advisor is Prof. Zhenyu GU. My research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence and design, aiming to understand the underlying logic of how content information drives design form. I have conducted in-depth work in areas such as intelligent and computational design, interaction design, and multimodal design resource retrieval. I am also interested in AI-empowered UX design and plan to further explore this direction.
